SEA COLLEGE OF Engineering

Welcome to S.E.A College of Engineering & Technology The South East Asian Education Trust Was Established in the yer 2000 with them main objective to provide academic excellence to all sections of the society. The trust focus on imparting quality education, disciplined integrated personality development, research, management skills to face the global challenges. S.E.A College of Engineering and Technology (SEACET) is established by Founder Chairman Sri A.Krishnappa in the year 2007 under SEAET, offering world-class infrastructure and the Management is keen to uphold the traditional Indian values. The thrust here is to blend thoroughly the modern and the traditional. An effort is afoot in the institution not only to raise the self-esteem of the student but to train him to respect others also.
